SABLOT

(Corypha utan)

The Sablot tree, scientifically known as Corypha utan (or occasionally Corypha elata), is a large palm native to the Philippines and Southeast Asia. It can grow up to 20 meters (about 66 feet) tall, featuring a thick trunk and a crown of large, fan-shaped leaves
